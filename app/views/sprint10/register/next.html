What happens next?

Maybe something like this:

1. The user "expresses an interest" in the standard and provider - offline or online. They provide some details to the provider - number of apprentices, contact info etc

2. A negotiation happens offline - price, start dates etc

3. When they agree that they want to go forward. A "formal" quote comes back into the "DAS" system - linked to the employers account so that we have a record of this. At this stage it may or may not contain named apprentices

4. Named apprentices can be assigned to this "quote" - where do they come from? piped in from another source or typed into this page on the DAS?

5. The "quote" is accepted into the DAS system and is then used to set up payments etc. Other things would have to happen offline before this can happen
