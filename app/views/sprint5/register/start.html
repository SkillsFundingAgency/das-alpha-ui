Get access to your funds

You can register for an account if you are a company that pays the apprenticeship levy.

Only employers who have a payroll of more than Â£3 million pay the apprenticeship levy.

Find out more about the apprenticeship levy and whether you need to create an account.

You can use the account to:

  • Get access to your funds
  • Find out how much you have to spend
  • Spend your funds on apprenticeships
  • Invite others in your organisation to view and spend funds

Before you start

You will need to have access to the following information to access your funds:

  • Knowledge of which PAYE schemes you want to associate with this account
  • A login to the Government Gateway accounts associated with the PAYE schemes (payroll will usually have this)
