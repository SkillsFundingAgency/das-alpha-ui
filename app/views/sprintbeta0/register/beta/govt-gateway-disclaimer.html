Permission to access PAYE information from HMRC

Information about the funding available to your organisation is held by HMRC on the Government Gateway.

The Digital Apprenticeship Service (run by the Skills Funding Agency) would like to access this information so that you can view your funding information in your apprenticeship account.

If you agree, your apprenticeship account will be given details of the funding available for your PAYE Schemes, but no other information about the PAYE schemes.

The apprenticeship account will also be given information about termination dates for the PAYE Schemes.

Do you agree to allow your Apprenticeship account to access PAYE information from HMRC.
