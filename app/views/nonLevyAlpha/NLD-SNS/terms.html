Terms

If you are considering employing an apprentice, use this service to find:

  • An apprenticeship that sets out the skills, knowledge and behaviours needed for the role.
  • An organisation to provide training for your apprentice.

Your responsibilities

Apprentices must be aged 16 or over and combine working with learning. They can be new or current employees.

If you hire an apprentice you must:

  • employ them for at least 30 hours a week
  • pay them at least the minimum wage for apprentices
  • offer them the same conditions and benefits as other employees working at a similar level
  • provide experience and opportunities to help with future job progression
  • allow them to attend training and assessment during their working hours
