On-site and Off-site training information

Training can be delivered to you and your apprentice in 3 main ways. You should look for the 'training structure' that suits your business best.

On-site training

On-site training is where your provider comes to your site and provides the training and assessment in the work place. This redcues the time the apprentice is out of the working environment but can cause disruption to the working day.

Off-site (block) training

Off-site there are two types of off-site training, the first being block training. Block training involves your apprentice spending a period of time (days, weeks) away in one block from the office.

Off-site (day release) training

Off-site day release training as the name suggests allows the apprentice to spend 1 day away from the work place with the training provider.
