Add and update apprentice records as a training provider

This service is for training providers that are registered with the Apprenticeship Service to:

  • respond to apprentice training requests from employers
  • add and update details of apprentices they're training

Before you start

You need to have an account with the government's Information Management Services system (Idams) to use this.

You'll get your account details when you register as an apprenticeship training provider.

Start now

You can contact the Apprenticeship Service for advice or help using the service.
