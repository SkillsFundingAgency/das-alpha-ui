Add an apprentice

Use this service to:

  • add a new apprentice to your account
  • make sure your training provider gets paid

You will need to know:

  • the UKPRN (UK provider reference number) of your training provider
  • who your apprentice will be employed by, if your business has more than one legal entity

You can choose to add details of each apprentice or allow your training provider to add details for you.

Start

Before you start

You must make sure you have:

  • an agreement with the Skills Funding Agency
  • a training agreement with your training provider
  • recruited and know the names of your new apprentices
