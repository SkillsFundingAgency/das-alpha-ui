Help with choosing a strong password

Choose a password with at least eight characters a combination of upper and lower case letters, numbers and keyboard symbols such as @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) _ +. (for example SP1D3Rm@n – a variation of spiderman, with letters, numbers, upper and lower case).

Never give your passwords to anyone else. If you think that someone else knows your password, change it immediately.
